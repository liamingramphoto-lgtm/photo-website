  • A Bit About Me

    I've simply always had a passion for three things, photography, music and film. And after spending ten years working as a graphic, web and advert designer for businesses up and down the country, Liam Ingram - Photo and Video was born. I now take photos and videos at the same time, so you don't need to hire both.

    I would describe my style as discreet in practice, and my approach is quite relaxed. I just try to capture all the memorabe moments that you'd love to see and cherish.

    I wont be posing you much or getting in your way, apart from a short intimate walk. We'll end up with beautiful shots and footage of both you and your guests. Although my style is candid, you'll find your video will be quite cinematic.

    While shooting a commercial for a business or product, I use quick but intuitive ways to film, creating depth and movement, making it more effective and easier to market your video. I have steadicams, sliders and lighting.

  • People & Portraits

    My passion is capturing the most unforgettable moments. I would say I'm described by clients as professional but friendly. Overall, you can expect a healthy mix of fun artistic candid shots as well as formals.

    Events & Clubs

    I've often been resident photographer and videographer for nightclubs, main events and shows. Clubs are very challenging, due to extreme low light, strobes and smokey effects, I've used this workspace to really hone in on my technical craft.

    Local & Commercial

    I'm often seen throughout North Tyneside and Newcastle with a camera in my hand, photographing local businesses, buildings, landscapes and capturing some of the most amazing scenes our region has to offer.
